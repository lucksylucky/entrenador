Playing style

I have a very practical style and am especially good in technical and strategic positions. I'm also quite an expert at thinking "outside the box". Also, I love chess endgames. I'll show you not to fear those "dead draws" and to identify and work with small strategic advantages in all kinds of positions. I've been experimenting with a more aggressive and concrete game style for the last few years, so I am a very complete player.

Endgames

I am quite an expert in this area. I understand them at a level way above my rating. I can show you how to understand them well so that you can score exceptionally well in equal positions. Controlling this final part of the game is also vital to make informed decisions in the middle game, such as what pieces to exchange or what endgame is more winnable or holdable.
